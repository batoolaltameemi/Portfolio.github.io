About Batool

Batool Al Tameemi is a 2004 artist (19 Years Old) of Iraqi heritage living in the UAE. Batool’s work revolves around expressionism and impressionism yet explores other art methods. She is interested in the ways humans convey different emotions and feelings and has this reflected on her canvases. Her work touches on many social themes and contests. She has been a part of many art programs in the UAE which made her today an art instructor. Batool presented more than 30 art workshops to many students as the number of her students exceeded 1000. Batool was awarded for her art by many local art contests including Painting for Palestine contest, Qasr al hosn the eyes of youth contest and many other contests, and has exhibited her work at multiple galleries in the UAE. She studies today at New York University Abu Dhabi and works as an art instructor at Sharjah children’s centres, she is also the print studio monitor at New York University Abu Dhabi
